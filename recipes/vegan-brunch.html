Vegan Brunch Recipe

Description

Meat Lovers, move aside... There's a new brunch sheriff in town, and this one didn't murder anything (or anyone). Hang on to your hats and get a load of this recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Richmond Vegan Sausages
  • 4 Richmond Vegan Bacon Rashers
  • 1 Large Avocado
  • 2 Slices, Sourdough
  • 400g Tin of Beans
  • Rocket (handful)
  • 6 Hash Browns

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200.C on fan.
  2. Add Hashbrowns into oven on Baking tray and cook for 20 Minutes or until golden brown. Turn halfway through cooking.
  3. Heat oil in pan and add sausages for 5 minutes, at which point, add the bacon and cook for a further 5 minutes.
  4. Slice Avocado into bowl and mash, mixing in your choice of seasoning.
  5. Put Sourdough slices in Toaster and toast for 4 minutes.
  6. Pour beans into bowl, season and microwave for 2 minutes, stiring halfway through.
  7. Serve on plates and garnish with Rocket.
