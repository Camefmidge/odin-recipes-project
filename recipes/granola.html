Granola Recipe

Description

Short, Sweet and Simple. This recipe is great for if you're on the go, on the go or on the go.

Ingredients:

  • Granola
  • Yoghurt
  • Mixed Fruit of your choice

Steps:

  1. Wash the fruit
  2. Pour Granola into bowl
  3. Pour Yoghurt onto Granola
  4. Pour Fruit onto Yoghurt
  5. Pour contents of bowl into mouth
